OUR MISSION

Donations are often pocketed by high - level officers, instead of being put towards a meaningful cause. We want to transparentize the virtual wall between donors and their donations, making it so donors are comfortable knowing that their money was used for its intended purposes.

OUR PROCESS

Donors - Step 1: The experience of Clharity begins with a donation through our website, which ends up in an inflation - free storage system. A small transaction fee is deducted from the donation. Clharity begins to monitor the finances of the charity and ensuring the money has been used for its intended purposes.

Donors - Step 2: Donors are notified as soon as their donations have been spent and explain the gracious impact of their donation. A filtered document is sent detailing the usage of the money. In the event that charities use the donation for an unintended purpose, Clharity will inform donors of that.

Charities - The first step is reaching an agreement about the best-intended use of the funds donated to them. After a listing has been established, we begin to ensure their transparency as they list on our site as an official charity. We present the charity with our exclusive and highly coveted benefits.
